# TaxEasy — Startup Description

## One-liner
**TaxEasy is an AI agent on WhatsApp that gives every Indian small-business owner their own CA — tax, GST, and bills help, in their own language.**

## Short (≈50 words)
TaxEasy is an AI-operated tax & finance assistant that lives in WhatsApp. Indian small-business owners — kirana shops, freelancers, doctors, gig workers, traders — message it in Hindi, Hinglish, or English to handle tax, GST, bills, and deadlines. A hard-coded engine does the math (never a wrong number); the AI guides, reminds, and files-prep — autonomously.

## Medium (≈100 words)
India has 45M+ small businesses that can't afford a chartered accountant, so they overpay tax, miss GST deadlines, and get notices. TaxEasy fixes that with an AI agent that lives where they already are — WhatsApp. Owners chat in their own language (Hindi/Hinglish/English); the agent onboards them in four questions, then proactively guides them: it flags GST-registration thresholds, reminds before every deadline, catches overcharged bills, and prepares their month-end tax bill. A deterministic tax engine computes every number — the AI never hallucinates a figure — so it's trustworthy by design. ₹0 free tier for low earners; ₹1,999/year for the full CA. Built AI-native: the agent runs the service; the founder sets strategy.

## Category (Build with Gemini XPRIZE)
**Small Business Services** — "powering everyday businesses with tools to compete and win."

## What AI does vs. what the human does
- **AI (Gemini):** understands every message, detects language + business segment, explains tax/GST, reads bills (vision), drafts reminders, runs the conversation and onboarding — autonomously, 24/7, with no founder in the loop per message.
- **Deterministic engine (not AI):** every tax/GST number, so figures are correct by construction.
- **Founder:** strategy, compliance sign-off, and the parts the law requires a human to authorize (the user taps to file).

## Traction & model
WhatsApp-first distribution (where users already are); ₹200/mo (₹1,999/yr) with a free funnel tier; scale-to-zero infra so it's cash-positive after the first paying users.
