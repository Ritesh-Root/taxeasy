## 1 — Title
**TaxEasy** · *Your CA, now on WhatsApp.*
हर बिज़नेस का अपना CA. · Ritesh, solo founder · Build with Gemini XPRIZE 2026.

## 2 — The problem
- India has **45M+ small businesses**; most can't afford a CA (₹10–50k/year).
- They **overpay tax** (taxed on turnover, not profit), **miss GST/ITR deadlines**, and get **notices**.
- Existing tools (ClearTax/TaxBuddy) are English, app-based, and built for the salaried — not the kirana owner or gig worker.

## 3 — The insight
The help has to come to them — **in WhatsApp, in their language, proactively** — not as another app to learn. And it must be **trustworthy**: one wrong tax number kills it.

## 4 — The product
An **AI agent on WhatsApp**. Message it in Hindi/Hinglish/English →
- onboards in 4 questions, detects your business + scheme,
- answers tax/GST/bills questions,
- **proactively** flags GST thresholds, deadlines, savings,
- prepares your month-end tax bill.

## 5 — Live demo (the hero slide)
Show a real chat: Lakshmi (kirana, Hindi) onboards in Devanagari → gets a correct
₹0 estimate + a proactive GST-registration warning → asks "नोटिस आएगा?" → calm
reassurance. **All deterministic, no human, in Hindi.**

## 6 — Why it's trustworthy (the moat)
**AI explains; a hard-coded engine calculates.** Every ₹ figure comes from a
versioned FY2025-26 tax engine — the AI never produces a number, so hallucinated
tax is structurally impossible. 45 automated tests guard the math.

## 7 — AI-native operations (what the judges score)
- **AI runs the business:** understands messages, detects language/segment, reads
  bills (Gemini vision), guides, reminds — autonomously, per-message, no founder.
- **Founder does:** strategy + compliance sign-off. User taps to file (the law's part).
- Every AI decision is **logged** (evidence trail).

## 8 — Built for everyone
Kirana · Freelancer · Doctor · Gig worker · Creator · Trader — one agent, adapts
per user (language, literacy, tax scheme). Validated with 6 personas in simulation.

## 9 — Traction & validation
- Working product: onboarding → advisory → reminders, **multilingual**, 45 tests green.
- Client simulation: agent's "punt rate" cut from 33% → 21%; Hindi users fully served.
- [Insert real users + revenue evidence here as it lands.]

## 10 — Business model
- **Free** (₹0): bills + reminders + 1 estimate/yr — the funnel.
- **Pro** (₹1,999/yr ≈ ₹166/mo): full engine, GST, month-end bill.
- **Business** (₹4,999/yr): multi-GSTIN, invoicing, CA export.
- Scale-to-zero infra → cash-positive after the first paying users.

## 11 — Tech & moat
Gemini (Flash/Pro) on Google Cloud · Firebase · hard-coded tax engine ·
hexagonal architecture (zero-data-loss, swappable transport/AI/storage) ·
WhatsApp-first. Moat = trust (deterministic numbers) + distribution + adaptivity.

## 12 — Market & impact
45M small businesses × even ₹1,999/yr = a multi-thousand-crore TAM. Impact:
democratizes CA-grade tax help for the underserved, prevents penalties, frees
owners to run their business.
